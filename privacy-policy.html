Privacy Policy

Welcome to the privacy policy page of our website dedicated to business coaching. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your personal data.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") applies to all users of our website and governs the collection and use of personal information we obtain while you use our services. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

2. Information Collected

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide when registering or subscribing to our newsletter.
  • Usage Information: We may collect data about how you use our website, including IP address, browser type, pages you visit, and access times.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to collect information about your preferences and to enhance your experience on our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected may be used for:

  • Providing and improving our services;
  • Personalizing the user experience;
  • Contacting you regarding your inquiries or questions;
  • Sending informational newsletters and updates;
  • Analyzing the use of our website and identifying trends.

4. Disclosure of Information

We will not disclose your personal information to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • It is required by law or in accordance with legal procedures;
  • It is necessary to protect the rights and property of our website;
  • It is related to the transfer of business, when we may transfer your information along with other assets.

5. Protection of Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. You are responsible for securing your account and password.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information;
  • Request correction or deletion of your data;

  • Challenge the processing of your personal data.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to gather information about your behavior on our website. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may impact the functionality of the site.

8. Links to Other Sites

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of each site you visit.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this Policy at any time. All changes will be posted on this page, and you are responsible for regularly reviewing the policy. Your continued use of the website after changes are made will be considered your acceptance of the new Policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or comments regarding this Policy, please contact us. We will strive to respond to your inquiries as quickly as possible.

11. Applicable Law

This Policy is governed by the laws and is interpreted in accordance with them. Any disputes arising from this Policy will be resolved in court.

12. Data Usage Terms

We may use your information for trend analysis and to improve the quality of our services. We may also use aggregated data for marketing and research purposes.

13. Consent to Data Processing

By using our website, you consent to the processing of your personal information in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use our website.

14. Access and Correction of Data

You have the right to request access to your data and correct any inaccuracies. To do so, please contact us with your request.

15. Data Retention

We will store your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or for the period required by law.

16. Age Restrictions

Our website is intended only for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it.

17. Termination of Data Use

You may stop using our services at any time and request the deletion of your information. We will process your request in accordance with applicable laws.

18. Anonymity and Confidentiality

We place a high value on user anonymity. We do not collect personal data without your consent and only use it for the purposes outlined in this Policy.

19. Data Security Policy

We take all reasonable measures to protect your data. However, you should also monitor the security of your information and not disclose your password to third parties.

20. Notes and Comments

We may retain your comments and feedback to improve our service. However, we will not disclose your personal information for these purposes.

21. Use of Information for Research

We may use anonymous data for research and analysis purposes, which will help us improve the quality of the services we provide.

22. Disclaimer

Although we strive to ensure the security and confidentiality of your data, we cannot guarantee full protection from unauthorized access and other threats. You use our website at your own risk.

23. Conclusion

Thank you for choosing our website for business coaching. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. If you have any questions regarding this Policy, please feel free to contact us.
